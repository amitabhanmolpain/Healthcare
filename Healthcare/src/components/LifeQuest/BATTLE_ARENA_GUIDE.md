# 🎮 LifeQuest Battle Arena - NEW GAME SYSTEM

## Game Overview

**LifeQuest** has been completely redesigned to focus on a **personal demon fighting system**. Instead of completing generic daily quests, you now create personalized "bad guys" representing real challenges in your life and battle them to overcome them.

---

## 🎯 How It Works

### 1. **Define Your Enemies** (Bad Guys Page)
- Go to the **"Enemies"** tab
- Click **"Add New Enemy"** to create challenges you want to overcome
- Examples:
  - Your Boss
  - Addiction (smoking, drinking, social media)
  - Disrespect 
  - Anxiety
  - Procrastination
  - Low Self-Esteem
  - Negative Thinking

### 2. **Battle Your Enemies** (Battle Arena Page)
- Go to the **"Battle Arena"** tab
- Select an active enemy to fight
- Use 4 different attack types:
  - **Quick Strike** (15-25 damage) - Fast and reliable
  - **Power Hit** (25-40 damage) - Stronger impact
  - **Blazing Combo** (35-55 damage) - Major hit
  - **Mega Slash** (50-75 damage) - Ultimate damage

### 3. **Gain XP & Level Up**
- Each attack deals damage to your enemy
- You gain XP based on damage dealt
- Build a **combo** by attacking consecutive times for bonus damage
- Level up and unlock badges for achievements

### 4. **Track Progress**
- View your battle statistics on the **Progress** page
- See how many enemies you've conquered
- Unlock badges and achievements

---

## 🎮 Game Mechanics

### **Damage System**
- Base damage varies per attack type
- **Combo Bonus**: Each consecutive attack adds 10% bonus damage
- Health bars show enemy status in real-time

### **Enemy Stats**
- Each enemy starts with **150 HP**
- Health is displayed as you battle
- Defeated enemies move to "Conquered Challenges" section

### **XP & Leveling**
- Earn XP from every attack
- 100 XP per level
- Higher levels unlock badges and special achievements

### **Battle Log**
- See the last 3 actions in each battle
- Track damage dealt and remaining HP
- Victory messages when you defeat an enemy

## 🏆 Achievements & Badges

Unlock badges by:
- Winning your first battle
- Reaching specific levels
- Defeating multiple enemies
- Building impressive stats

## ⚔️ Example Gameplay

1. **Create Enemy**: "My Addiction to Coffee"
2. **Enter Battle**: See the enemy with health bar
3. **Attack**: Choose your attack type (e.g., "Blazing Combo" - 45 damage)
4. **Build Combo**: Keep attacking to build a combo multiplier
5. **Victory**: When enemy HP reaches 0, you win and gain XP
6. **Progression**: Move to next enemy or create new one

---

## 💡 Tips & Strategy

- **Combo attacks are powerful**: Keep attacking to build multiplier
- **Mix attack types**: Different enemies might need different strategies
- **Monitor health**: Watch your enemy's remaining HP in the battle log
- **Level up**: Higher levels = stronger attacks potential
- **Create realistic enemies**: Base them on real challenges for better motivation
